## How Lark uses this file

**Three ways a conference creates a signal or outreach opportunity:**

1. **Prospect org is presenting or on a panel** — signals governance
   maturity and sector engagement. Natural outreach hook. Bump to Score-1
   minimum; combine with other signals for compound scoring.

2. **Conference as signal amplifier** — a prospect with a fresh leadership
   change who is also presenting at a sector conference = high conviction
   reset moment. Compound this with SIG-001 or SIG-002 for Score-3.

3. **Conference as outreach timing** — knowing a prospect will be at a
   specific event gives the Farther team a warm, non-cold opener.
   Flag 6 weeks before each event so the team can reach out in advance.

**Monitoring question Lark asks:**
Are any of our HubSpot contacts presenting, attending, or mentioned
in connection with this event — and does that create a move window?

**Queries to run for each conference:**
- `"[conference name]" "[org name]" speaker OR presenter OR panelist [year]`
- `"[conference name]" "[org name]" attending OR registration [year]`
- `site:linkedin.com "[org name]" "[conference name]" [year]`

## Tier C — Regional Conferences
Small nonprofits — community foundations, local arts orgs, faith-based
organizations, social service nonprofits — attend regional conferences,
not national ones. Regional events are frequently overlooked but highly
relevant to Lark's pipeline.

Lark does not maintain a fixed list of regional conferences — there are
too many to track. Instead, Lark uses a query framework to find relevant
regional events for specific HubSpot contacts on demand.

### Regional conference query framework

When sweeping a contact in a specific geography, run:

```
"[state] nonprofit association" conference 2026
"[state] council of nonprofits" annual conference 2026
"[city] community foundation" conference OR convening 2026
"[region] philanthropy" conference 2026
AFP "[chapter name]" conference 2026
```

### Why regional conferences matter for compound scoring

A contact org attending a regional conference alone = Score-1 at most.
But a contact org whose new ED is speaking at the state nonprofit
association conference = SIG-002 (new ED) + conference engagement signal
= Score-2 minimum, possibly Score-3 if AUM threshold also applies.

The regional conference signals governance engagement from a new leader —
exactly the profile most receptive to a Farther conversation.

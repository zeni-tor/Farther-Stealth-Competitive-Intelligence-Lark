Since 1998 it has raised $383M+ and awarded 161,000+ scholarships across 38 schools. A newer arm, "Changing Lives with Legacy," accepts traditional charitable gifts directed to an Education Growth Fund and an Endowment for future scholarships. [Confirmed · catholiceducationarizona.org, catholicsun.org · retrieved 2026-06-24]
